# 44 - SME Interview and Debate Bank (Technical Communication Mastery)
Home: [README](../README.md)

Elite engineering requires the ability to explain and defend decisions under pressure.

## Bank structure
Maintain question sets in these domains:
- Python language internals
- API and service architecture
- data engineering and SQL design
- reliability and incident response
- security and compliance
- scaling and performance

## Practice protocol
1. Select 10 questions/week.
2. Answer in writing with examples.
3. Record spoken explanation (5 to 10 minutes).
4. Critique your own response for clarity and rigor.

## Pass criteria
- Answers are precise, not vague.
- Tradeoffs are explicit.
- Alternatives and risks are addressed.
